About

Hello, my name is Fidel Flores Cáceres. I am from San Salvador, El Salvador. I am currently pursuing a BS in Computational Media at the Georgia Institute of Technology with specializations in Intelligence and Interaction Design and Experimental Media. Besides coding, I enjoy cycling, playing racquetball, video gaming, reading up on tech articles, and learning!

As far as coding goes, I’ve always been interested and immersed in technology. As a kid, I would spend hours on the computer just dissecting its components and figuring out how a computer works - from understanding what a BIOS is to actually building a computer. It was all about the functionality and the hardware of a computer until I took my first coding class in 10th grade - an intermediate Java programming class. Little did I know that that class would be the foundation for all of my future coding endeavors.
